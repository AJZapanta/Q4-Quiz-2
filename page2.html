CHARACTERS IN NOLI ME TANGERE
Juan Crisostomo y Ibarra
  • He is described as wealthy young man of mixed Spanish and Filipino ancestry who has recently returned to the Philippines from Europe after spending seven years studying abroad.
  • He is an earnest, idealistic, cultured, and well-respected person.
Maria Clara de los Santos
  • She is the daughter of Kapitan Tiago and Dona Pia Alba, but she is actually the illegitimate daughter of Padre Damaso who engaged in sexual relations with the latter. She was raised by Tia Isabel in absence of Pia Alba, who died giving birth to her.
  • She is sweet, beautiful, kindhearted, and well-loved.
Padre Damaso
  • He is a bad Franciscan friar and he was a former curate of San Diego before being replaced by Padre Salvi. It was revealed to be the true father of Maria Clara, having engaged in sexual relations with Dona Pia Alba.
  • He is seen as arrogant person and acts superior to others with his power as friar.
Kapitan Tiago
  • He is a businessman in San Diego, the husband of Pia Alba, and the non-biological father of Maria Clara. He became a good influential businessman.
  • He is wealthy and has multiple social connections.
Padre Salvi
  • He is the current town curate of San Diego, being the replacement Padre Damaso.
  • He is seen as a manipulative friar and he is the rival of the alferez of the Guardian Civil, and he longs for Crisostomo's passing.
